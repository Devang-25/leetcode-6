## Bitwise Operators

* `&` and
* `|` or
* `^` xor
* `~` not
* `<<` left shift
* `>>` right shift

## Binary To Decimal

12 -> 0

12 % 2 = 0
6 % 2 = 0
3 % 2 = 1
1 % 2 = 1

1100

0 + 0 + 2^2 * 1 + 2^3 * 1 = 4 + 8 = 12
